Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Mochi

How does Mochi learn my preferences?
Mochi uses advanced machine learning algorithms to observe your daily routines, preferences, and interactions. Over time, it adapts to provide more personalized assistance and suggestions tailored specifically to your lifestyle.
What smart home systems does Mochi work with?
Mochi is compatible with most popular smart home ecosystems including Google Home, Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit, Samsung SmartThings, and many others. It can control lights, thermostats, security systems, and more.
Is my privacy protected with Mochi?
Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. All data is encrypted, and you have full control over what information Mochi collects. You can review, manage, or delete your data at any time through the companion app.
How often does Mochi receive updates?
Mochi receives regular over-the-air updates approximately every month. These updates include new features, performance improvements, and security enhancements to ensure your Mochi continues to improve over time.
What is the warranty period for Mochi?
The warranty period varies by model: Mochi Basic comes with a 1-year warranty, Mochi Pro with 2 years, and Mochi Elite with a lifetime warranty. All warranties cover manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions.
